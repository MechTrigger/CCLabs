Instructions

SPEEDCOINS: Click one of three boxes that contains the best answer for the value displayed. The correct answer will be the one that adds up to the correct change amount in as few coins as possible. Value will be between $.01 and $9.99. You have 10 seconds to pick the right answer. A correct answer will give you +10 points, a wrong answer -5 points. Get to 50 points to win the game. After you close the answer dialog box, timer will start again on the next problem. Have fun!

COIN CLICKER: Click the coins to get the correct change amount. Remember, it must be in the fewest coins as possible. From LEFT to RIGHT coins are as follows, SilverDollar 1.0, HalfDollar .50, Quarter .25, Dime .10, Nickle .05, Penny .01--You have 10 seconds to submit the right answer. A correct answer will give you +10 points, a wrong answer -5 points. Get to 50 points to win the game. After you close the answer dialog box, timer will start again on the next problem. Good luck!
