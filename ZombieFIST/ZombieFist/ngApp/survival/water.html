You Are Probably Thirsty

Water is the most important factor when it comes to long term suvival. Always know where you are and where the water is. Don't know how to read a map? You better learn because GPS probably won't work. You should have plenty of water purification tabs, kits, and worse case a pot to boil the water first. I wouldn't trust any source that isn't in a sealed bottle. You never know what kind of posse is lurking in there waiting to go all medival on your system. It's water. You need it. Find it. Drink it.
