Weapons of war

Weapon choice for building defense is dramtically more verstile than if you are trekking across zombieland. When weight isn't a factor you can afford to have tremendous variety. While we don't recommend playing a head blasting mash of celebrity look-a-likes meets where's waldo, you should have weapons to poke things at tremendous distance. It may just help keep the other most prevalent enemy at bay, other people.
    Top Ten Weapons and Why:
  • 9mm/40cal Pistol - Most readily available ammunition.
  • 12ga Shotgun - The most versatile weapon in your arsenal.
  • Bolt Action Rifle - For poking things from a distance.
  • Compound Bow - It pays to be quiet...shhhhhh...
  • Machete - For reasons other than splitting skulls, but that's cool too.
  • .50 Desert Eagle - Intimidation and the potential to use Bulletooth Tony lines all day.
  • AK47 - Reliability. Bury it for 5 years, dig it up, and it still shoots. Awesome.
  • Flamethrower - You honestly think you would find one? Still, it'd be awesome.
  • A1 Abrahams - Ha! Let's hope there is an instruction manual. Hella awesome.
  • Your Mind - "C'mon stop trying to hit me and hit me!" It will save you, for awhile.
